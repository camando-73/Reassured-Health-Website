What We Do

We put you in charge of your health by securing the transfer of protected health information and providing actionable insights to employers, healthcare providers, and County and State Health Departments.

Protected Health Information, or “PHI”, is the vital information about your health that allows you to understand your health and healthcare providers to know how to keep you healthy and safe. PHI is confidential and protected by the Health Information Portability and Accountability Act of 1996, more commonly referred to as “HIPAA”.

Reassured Health’s Mission is to empower individuals to take control of their health and their PHI, and to empower communities to stay healthy and manage outbreaks. When the right people have the right information, our communities will be safer and healthier, and we can keep our economy moving, even through crises such as the COVID-19 pandemic. It’s YOUR information. You can keep it private and make sure it is used to keep you and your Community healthier and safer. Reassured Health can help.

Who We Serve

Above all, we put individuals in charge of their own health and health information. Your protected health information belongs to you. Reassured Health serves the patient and the community first and foremost.

We also help County and State Health Departments keep their communities healthier by providing actionable insights relating to public health crises and outbreaks, such as COVID-19. Our innovative software solutions can geographically pinpoint areas of need, and our novel CV-Alert Outbreak Management System gives Counties and States the tools they need to mitigate outbreaks and manage the spread of contagious disease.

Our CV Alert Outbreak Management System allows employers to protect their employees and customers by allowing employers to track symptoms and receive real time notifications of testing and hospitalizations. Businesses across the United States are looking for ways to remain open while doing everything they can to mitigate the spread of COVID-19. Employers who use CV Alert Outbreak Management System are going above and beyond to protect their employees, customers and communities.

Our Products

CV Alert

CV Alert is an Outbreak Management System which gives employers and communities the necessary tools to quickly identify and track COVID-19-related symptoms, testing and hospitalizations with the full consent of the patient.


The health of our communities is intertwined with the health of our economy. But in these uncertain and dangerous times, simply opening the doors to your business can have devastating consequences. But with CV Alert, employers and business owners, have a tool that allows them track temperatures and loss of smell by employee over time and soon, get real time notices of instances of testing, test results, and hospitalizations of employees of their family members. In so doing, CV Alert provides unprecedented security for your PHI while giving communities the tools to keep their economy moving. This means outbreaks can be managed, people can go to work, and we can move more quickly down the road to Recovery

Why CV Alert?

Are you doing everything you can to keep your employees and customers safe? You can with CV Alert.

Only 30% of people who are COVID-19 positive have a fever. Approximately 60% of COVID-19 positives people lose their sense of smell. CV Alert not only provides smell tests to supplement the taking of temperatures, but allows employers to receive critical, life-saving information in real time so they can decide how to operate safely. Employers utilizing CV Alert can also track symptoms and testing over time to further minimize the risk of an outbreak.

Most employers are taking temperatures. But CV Alert tracks not only temperatures but smells test over time, providing valuable and ACTIONABLE insights to manage outbreaks.

Communities and health organizations face major hurdles in obtaining and effectively analyzing comprehensive health information from the many health-related entities and organizations who serve a given community. Vital data is often incomplete, inaccurate, or simply unavailable. Reassured Health is leveraging its relationships throughout healthcare and government and developing unique and powerful software solutions to bring this data to those organizations, providers, first responders, and employers who can then use it to keep their communities safer, healthier and happier.

How we Protect your Health Information

Your health information belongs to you! Reassured Health is fully committed to complete HIPAA compliance. We obtain full patient consent to provide secured transfers of symptom tracking, testing and hospitalization data related to COVID-19. This allows only those legally entitled to the information to receive actionable insights which can help manage and mitigate outbreaks, allow us to keep our economy open, and most importantly, save lives.

About Us

Reassured Health, LLC was founded by the winners of Rio Grande Valley Health Information Exchange’s 2019 Hackathon (Project Rapitrack). Since the Hackathon, we have been invested in the solution that we presented, Rapitrack, an EMS-Hospital handoff solution, later renamed to Prime Axon (Patient Record Intelligence for Medical Emergencies –Actionable eXchanges Over Network). However, since the emergence of the COVID19 pandemic, our priorities have shifted into creating a tool to help better monitor and mitigate the spread of this devastating disease.
